The problem

Generic chatbots weren't built to sell.

01
Off-the-shelf bots can't see your live inventory or order status — they guess, or send the shopper straight to a human.
02
Cart and checkout questions are time-sensitive — every minute a shopper waits is a minute closer to abandoning.
03
Generic AI tools don't understand SKUs, variants, or return policies — they're built for support tickets, not storefronts.
