Use cases

Built for the shopping
journey, category by category.

Every Wrennon agent is trained on a real catalog and real order data. Here's what that looks like across ecommerce categories — and beyond.

Fashion & apparel

Sizing, stock & style matching

Answers sizing and fit questions against your actual size charts, checks live stock by variant, and suggests alternatives when something's sold out.

Electronics & gadgets

Specs, compatibility & warranty

Handles spec comparisons and "will this work with my setup" questions using your real product data — plus warranty and return eligibility.

Home & lifestyle

Order tracking & delivery

Resolves "where's my order" and delivery-window questions directly against your fulfillment data, before the shopper has to ask twice.

Beauty & personal care

Product matching & reorders

Recommends products from your catalog based on shopper needs, and makes repeat ordering a one-message conversation on WhatsApp.

Food & D2C brands

Subscriptions & cart recovery

Manages subscription changes and follows up on abandoned carts at the right moment — without sounding like an automated blast.

Beyond ecommerce

Internal ops & service intake

Once your storefront agent is live, the same engine extends to internal tools, service intake, and other operational workflows.

By stage

Same agent, every stage of the funnel.

PRE-PURCHASE
Answers product questions — sizing, stock, compatibility — using your live catalog, before doubt turns into a lost sale.
CHECKOUT
Clears last-minute friction — shipping cost, delivery windows, payment options — right when the shopper is about to drop off.
POST-PURCHASE
Handles order status and returns end to end, pulled live from your store — no "let me check and get back to you."
RETENTION
Follows up on abandoned carts and past orders at the right moment — a nudge, a question answered, or a reorder made easy.
