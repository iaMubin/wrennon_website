How it connects

Three steps, not a migration.

Nothing about your existing store changes. The agent connects alongside it.

I.

Connect your store

Share read access to your platform — catalog, orders, and policies. No code changes on your end.

II.

We sync & train

Products, variants, stock, and order data sync in. The agent is trained on your policies and brand tone.

III.

Go live on your channels

Deploy to web chat, WhatsApp, or both. Stock and order data stay live — no manual updates needed.

→
Data syncs on a schedule you set — near real-time for stock and order status, so answers never lag behind your store.
→
Access is read-first by default — the agent only writes where you explicitly allow it, like updating an order note or issuing store credit.
→
